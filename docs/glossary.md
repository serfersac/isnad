
# Technical Glossary for [counterspec/isnad]

## Definitions

### Inscription
An inscription in this context refers to the process of recording and submitting data to the protocol's blockchain. This can include transactions, attestations, or other relevant information that needs to be permanently stored and verified on the chain.

### Attestation
An attestation is a cryptographically signed statement made by a validator or node that confirms the validity of a particular state or event. Attestations are crucial for maintaining the integrity and consensus of the protocol.

### Slashing
Slashing is a penalty mechanism applied to validators who behave maliciously or violate the protocol's rules. When slashing occurs, a portion of the validator's staked tokens is forfeited as a deterrent against misbehavior.

### Trust Tier
Trust Tier represents a classification system for validators based on their reliability, performance, and history of honest operation. Validators are categorized into different tiers to reflect their level of trustworthiness within the network.

### Auditor Jury
The Auditor Jury is a group of designated validators or nodes responsible for monitoring and auditing the activities of other validators. They play a critical role in ensuring the protocol's security and integrity by detecting and reporting potential malicious behavior.
